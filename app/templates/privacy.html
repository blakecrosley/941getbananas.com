Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2025

Get Bananas ("the App") is developed by 941 Apps. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Summary: Get Bananas does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your shopping list stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data shared with third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Shopping List Items: The items you add to your shopping list, including item names, quantities, sections, and checked status.
  • App Preferences: Your chosen language and display settings.
  • Crash Diagnostics: If the app crashes, diagnostic information is stored locally for up to 30 days to help improve app stability. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, the App can sync your shopping list across your Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch) using iCloud Drive.

  • Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, protected by your Apple ID.
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data.
  • iCloud sync is controlled by your device settings and can be disabled at any time.
  • Apple's iCloud privacy policy applies to data stored in iCloud.

Claude Desktop Integration (macOS)

The macOS version of Get Bananas includes an optional integration with Claude Desktop via the Model Context Protocol (MCP).

  • This feature is entirely optional and must be manually installed.
  • When enabled, Claude Desktop can read and modify your shopping list stored locally on your Mac.
  • Your shopping list data may be sent to Anthropic's servers when you interact with Claude Desktop. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to that interaction.
  • This integration does not affect the privacy of the iOS or watchOS versions of the app.

Third-Party Services

Get Bananas does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Get Bananas does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
